Our Mission

At Refresh, “We strive to give our clients added comfort and connection within the community through a consistently excellent barbershop and salon experience.”

Why Choose Us

There are a lot of barbershops out there. Why choose Refresh?

Convenient

Located directly on the South40, getting a quick cut has never been quicker and easier. 

Consistent

With licensed STL barbers, we deliver on our promise to provide consistently quality cuts to our clients. 

Comfortable

We want your time inside our shop to be an enjoyable one, so we do everything we can to make our space inviting for our clients.
